About

John Adetola is a backend developer and circuit designer based in Abuja, Nigeria. In 2013, John, who was doing his diploma at the time, designed a power supply, battery charger, continuity tester and automatic water controller which kicks started his circuit design prowess.

John's back-end development started in the year 2019, with C# and later into Python in the year 2020. He is committed in making new and better designs and applications.

With his interactive ability, he is able to communicate with the users to detect the discover problems in other to design things to their comfortabilty.
